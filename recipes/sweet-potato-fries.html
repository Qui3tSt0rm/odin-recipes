Sweet Potato Fries

Crispy Golden Sweet Potato Fries

These sweet potato fries are a great side dish for burgers or other sandwiches. They can be made salty or spicy and served with a side of ketchup or bbq sauce.

The best sweet potato fries are cut thin so that they cook evenly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 or 3 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Optional spices: freshly ground black pepper, cayenne pepper and/or garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Slice fries thinly into 1/4 inches wide
  2. Toss sliced fries in cornstarch before oil
  3. Divide fries between two pans and arrange them in even layers
  4. Bake at 425 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes, mixing halfway through
  5. Season with pepper, garlic powder, and/or cinnamon
