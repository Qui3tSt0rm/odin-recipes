Cauliflower Crust Pizza

Cauliflower crust pizza is a great low-carb pizza option. You can make it dairy free as well and add as many toppings as you want.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of riced cauliflower
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozerella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teapoon of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • Toppings - for example - pepperoni and garlic

Instructions:

  1. Thaw riced cauliflower and press with paper towels to get rid of moisture.
  2. In a bowl, mix cauliflower, mozerella, parmesan, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano.
  3. Lightly beat eggs and add them to the cauliflower mixture.
  4. Spread the mixture on a baking pan until completely flat and even and bake at 400 degrees F for 25 minutes.
  5. Remove crust from oven and add pizza sauce, cheese, and toppings. Bake for an additional 10 minutes and enjoy!
