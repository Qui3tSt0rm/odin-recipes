Classic Stuffed Bell Peppers

Multicolored bell peppers stuffed with ground beef, beans and cheese.

These classic stuffed bell peppers are a great low-carb option that are filling and taste great.

Ingredients:

  • 6 bell peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Taco Seasoning
  • Ground beef
  • Beans
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Wash bell peppers and cut off tops, remove seeds and membranes.
  2. Cut peppers in half and place on a baking tray. Season with salt and pepper and add olive oil. Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes.
  3. While peppers are in oven, heat ground beef in a pan under medium heat until brown. Stir in beans, taco seasoning and a little big of water, stir to coat.
  4. When peppers are done cooking, add beef mixture and top with mozerella cheese. Put back in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven. Add optional garnishes such as jalepeno peppers or hot sauce.
